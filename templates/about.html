About Voices of Artsakh

Voices of Artsakh is a digital documentation and education project dedicated to preserving first-hand testimonies from those connected to Artsakh. The archive centers human stories and makes them accessible for students, educators, and global audiences.

Motivation

The project began after hearing classmates describe displacement, loss, and resilience firsthand. These personal accounts demonstrated how easily lived experiences can be lost without careful preservation. The archive ensures these voices remain visible, respected, and meaningful.

What makes the platform different

  • Verified, curated testimonies with ethical consent.
  • Structured learning paths supported by an AI guide.
  • Subtitled Armenian-language videos for international accessibility.

Long-term vision

The project aims to expand into a broader archive for other communities affected by conflict and displacement, building a global collection of testimonies and educational resources.
